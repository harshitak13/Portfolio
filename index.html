Research

Published Work

Manuscript Under Peer Review
SafeGAT — LLM-Augmented Graph Attention
Reinforcement Learning Framework

An adaptive traffic signal control system that fuses the structural reasoning of Graph Attention Networks with the generalisation capabilities of Large Language Models. SafeGAT outperforms pure RL baselines on unseen road topologies — demonstrating that LLM-derived auxiliary policy guidance can substantially improve zero-shot transfer in multi-agent traffic environments.
